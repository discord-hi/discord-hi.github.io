What is hi?
hi is a giveaway Discord Guild/Website where free stuff is given away.
What kind of free stuff?
Steam games, Amazon gift cards, PayPal cash, Google Play Store gift cards, iTunes gift cards, and sooooooo much more.
Why do you give away "free stuff"? What's the catch?
The catch is that you have to be social, and participate in our community discussions (yes, that's right, we're a social server too).
We do not display ads on any of our pages, sell your data in any way (we don't even collect your data), or mine any cryptocurrency on your computer.
Why do you need both the website and Discord?
We have way more control over giveaways when using a website we made. We use Discord as it allows for easily communicating with other people.
Our main goal is just to have large Discord server where people just be social (sometimes it's all about the numbers).
You don't have a "contact us" section on this website. How do I contact you?
You can contact us by joining hi or DMing us on Discord (NotTacoFrost#2938 and LittleWhole#1337).
